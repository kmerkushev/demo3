Why is the Timing Right?

  1. Growing Real-World Adoption: The sector is gaining significant traction, highlighted by successful initiatives such as Helium Mobile surpassing 120k subscribers in its first year, Glow deploying 70 solar farms across regions from California to India, and GEODNET establishing the world’s largest RTK network with over 11k nodes.
  2. Surge in Early-Stage Fundraising: In 2024, DePIN projects experienced explosive growth in funding, increasing 326.45% compared to 2023. Key areas like compute, energy, and data collection attracted over $266 million in total investment.
  3. Liquid Market Signals a Breakout: 2024 was a pivotal year for DePIN, with the sector's total market capitalization climbing 132% year-over-year, exceeding $40 billion.
  4. Rapid Revenue Growth: Protocols are beginning to generate substantial revenue, signaling demand-side traction previously absent. GLOW surpassed $25 million in revenue, while Helium and IO.net both exceeded $12 million. Other notable performers include Filecoin ($8 million), Render ($4 million), Akash ($4 million), and GEODNET ($2 million), all surpassing $1 million in annual recurring revenue (ARR).
